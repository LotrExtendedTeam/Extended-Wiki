# Gundabad Uruk

Gundabad Uruks are the greatest warriors of the Orc-tribes of the Misty Mountains, far deadlier than the lesser Gundabad Orcs they often lead into battle. While they look like ordinary Orcs at a glance, Gundabad Uruks are larger and have all the strengths of the Uruk-hai, most notably greater tolerance for sunlight.
Gundabad Uruks wear Gundabad Uruk armour and wield Gundabad Uruk weapons, which are just as strong as Dwarven armour and weapons, allowing them to deal considerable damage and endure plenty of punishment. As Gundabad's greatest champions, Gundabad Uruks bear the Shield of Filth, painted crudely with the symbol of the Red Eye.

## Spawning

Gundabad Uruks spawn by night in the Misty Mountains, Ettenmoors, Coldfells, and Grey Mountains, as well as in Gundabad invasions or biomes that have been conquered by Gundabad.
Typically, they spawn in groups, though these groups aren't as large as bands of common Orcs.

## Behaviour

Gundabad Uruks will attack any foes of Gundabad, be they players with negative Gundabad alignment or enemy NPCs. When not in combat, they will wander aimlessly like the other Gundabad Orcs.

## Strategy 

- They are the strongest Gundabad units, as well as their top-notch armour and weaponry, so be equiped with good gear.
- Uruk soldiers are almost always accompanied by Uruk archers, so bringing a shield into battle is mandatory.
- Try to draw them away and fight them one at a time, because many Uruks can overwhelm you, especially with insufficient equipment.

## Lore

Gundabad Orcs recieved their name for the sacking of a dwarven mountain fortress named Mount Gundabad during the Second Age.
