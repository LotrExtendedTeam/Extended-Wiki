# Blue Mountains Dwarf

Blue Mountain Dwarves are a common sight across the Blue Mountains. They spawn randomly as well as in Blue Mountains Strongholds and Settlements.

## Behavior

Dwarves will attack when a fellow kinsman is killed in their vacinity.

## Hiring

They can be hired for 30 coins from [[blue_mountains_commander|Blue Mountains Commanders]] when one's aignment is >=200 with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].
