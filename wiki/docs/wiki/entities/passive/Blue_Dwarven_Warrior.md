# Blue Mountains Warrior

Blue Mountain Warriors are a common sight across the Blue Mountains. They spawn randomly as well as in Blue Mountains Strongholds and Settlements.

## Behavior

Warriors will attack opposing faction npcs as well as players with less than 0 alignment with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].

## Hiring

They can be hired for 40 coins from [[blue_mountains_commander|Blue Mountains Commanders]] when one's aignment is >=250 with the [[blue_mountain_faction|Blue Mountains]].
