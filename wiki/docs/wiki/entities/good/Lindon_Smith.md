# Lindon Smith

 The Lindon Smith is a trader that specializes in Lindon Armour and Tool/Weapons, found in Lindon Villages.

## Spawning

The Lindon Smith spawns in the Lindon Village, which randomly generates inside the Lindon Biome. The Smith can be found in the village smithy(not always present) or in the village marketplace.
  
## Behavior

The Lindon Smith will not attack any player or creature until they are attacked.
They wander around their smithy/market booth when not be traded with.
Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

The Lindon Smith fight enemies with a Blacksmith's Hammer. They have no armor, making them easy kills, although the hammer's high damage and knockback could take an unarmored player by surprise.

## Trading

Smiths will not trade with players unless they have +100 alignment with [[high_elves_faction|High Elves]]. Upon trading with then, the player will be bestowed the advancement "**Elder Craftmanship**".

Lindon Smiths sell the goods of their trade. This includes Lindon armor and equipment. They also can sell the Lindon crafting table and Banner Pattern.

Lindon Smiths buy many supplies, such as ingots and gemstones.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Lindon Smith, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack.

## Lore

The Rivendell Elves are Noldorin Elves who dwell in Rivendell, home of the Lord Elrond. They are more ancient than the Wood-Elves and Galadhrim, and are great warriors and loremasters.
