# Bree-land Brewer

The Bree-land Brewer is a trader that specializes in alcoholic beverages.

## Spawning

The Bree-land Brewer spawns in the brewery, found in Bree-land villages.
  
## Behavior

Bree-land Brewers will wander around their brewery when not selling goods. Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

Bree-land Brewers fight with any of the following: Iron and bronze daggers and iron, bronze and stone axes.
  
## Trading

Brewers will not trade with players if they have negative alignment with Bree-land. Upon trading with them, the player will be bestowed the advancement **"Insider Bargains"**.

Brewers sell the goods of their trade. This includes mugs and barrels of varying drinks.

Bree-land Brewers also buy many supplies, such as food and drink ingredients.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Bree-land Brewer, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack, just watch out for their axes!

## Lore

The Bree-land people claim to be descendants of the first men who went west and served Morgoth.
