# Lindon Weaver

 The Lindon Weaver is a trader that specializes in leather armor and paintings, found in Lindon Villages.
 
## Spawning

The Lindon Weaver spawns in the Lindon Village, which randomly generates inside the Lindon Biome. The Weaver can be found in the village marketplace.
  
## Behavior

The Lindon Weaver will not attack any player or creature until they are attacked.
They stand in their market booth when not be traded with.
Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

The Lindon Weaver fight enemies with a Lindon Dagger. They have no armor, making them easy kills.

## Trading

Weavers will not trade with players unless they have +50 alignment with [[high_elves_faction|High Elves]].

Lindon Weavers sell the goods of their trade. This includes leather armor and paintings.

Lindon Weavers buy many supplies, such as leather and dyes.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Lindon Weaver, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack.

## Lore

The Laiquendi (Lindon) Elves or Green-Elves are a clan of Nandorin Elves that crossed the Blue Mountains before abandoning the Great Journey to Valinor.
