# Lindon Warriors

LIndon Warriors are the guards of the fair realm of Lindon on the West Coast, protecting the only escape from Middle-Earth until their turn comes.

## Spawning

Lindon Warriors can spawn any time of the day, within the Lindon biome and in the Tower Hills Towers.
  
## Behavior

Lindon Warriors will attack any player or creature aligned with an evil faction, although their location means they rarely fight naturally.
They generally wander around aimlessly when they are not fighting enemies.

## Equipment

LIndon Warriors fight enemies with a Lindon Bow and a random Lindon melee weapon, which are only the sword and spear at the moment. When hired they have full armor(some unired warriors spawn without helmets), making them afforable and very strong combat units, although the alignment requirment is quite high.
  
## Hiring

They can be hired for 45 coins from a [[lindon_lord|Lindon Lord]] when one's alignment is >=400 with the [[high_elves_faction|High Elves]].

## Strategy

- When fighting Lindon Warriors or any other ranged enemy, a shield is extremely useful to block arrows.
- Fighting Elven Warriors is almost always a bad idea: They have a strong ranged attack and full armor, so if you want to fight them, you better bring an army.
- If you are fighting larger numbers, try to draw them towards you one by one, as a large group can overwhelm you with arrows very quickly.

## Lore

The Rivendell Elves are Noldorin Elves who dwell in Rivendell, home of the Lord Elrond. They are more ancient than the Wood-Elves and Galadhrim, and are great warriors and loremasters.
