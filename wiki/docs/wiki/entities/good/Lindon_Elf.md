# Lindon Elf

Lindon Elves are the inhabitants of the Elven realm of Lindon, The far west coastland split by the Gulf of Lhûn into the regions of Forlindon and Harlindon.

## Spawning

Lindon Elves can spawn any time of the day, within the Lindon and Tower Hills Biomes.
  
## Behavior

Lindon Elves will attack any player or creature aligned with an evil faction, although thier locaction means they rarely fight naturally.
They generally wander around aimlessly when they are not fighting enemies.

## Equipment

Lindon Elves fight enemies with a Lindon Bow and Dagger. They have no armor, making them cheap, but weak combat units.
  
## Hiring

They can be hired for 30 coins from a [[lindon_lord|Lindon Lord]] when one's alignment is >=300 with the [[high_elves_faction|High Elves]].

## Strategy

- When fighting Lindon Elves or any other ranged enemy, a shield is extremely useful to block arrows.
- Try and get close enough for them to immediately switch to their melee weapon, which toggles on at shorter range. If you are at the right distance, they can't damage you with it.
- If you are fighting larger numbers, try to draw them towards you one by one, as a large group can overwhelm you with arrows very quickly.

## Lore

The Laiquendi (Lindon) Elves or Green-Elves are a clan of Nandorin Elves that crossed the Blue Mountains before abandoning the Great Journey to Valinor.
