# Rivendell Weaver

 The Rivendell Weaver is a trader that specializes in leather armor and paintings, found in Rivendell Weavers Houses.

## Spawning

The Rivendell Weaver spawns in the Rivendell Weaver House, indentified by the classic A-frame shape and flax garden, which randomly generates inside the Rivendell Biome.
  
## Behavior

The Rivendell Weaver will not attack any player or creature untiled they are attacked.
They wander around their house when not be traded with. 
Upon death, a silver coin will float above the ground where they died, signifying that they will respawn here after a short period of time.

## Equipment

The Rivendell Weaver fight enemies with a Rivendell Dagger. They have no armor, making them easy kills.

## Trading

Weavers will not trade with players unless they have +50 alignment with [[high_elves_faction|High Elves]].

Rivendell Weavers sell the goods of their trade. This includes leather armor and paintings.

Rivendell Weavers buy many supplies, such as leather and dyes.

## Strategy

- There is no need to fight a Rivendell Weaver, but if you do, it is easy to kill them with a longer weapon such as a spear, or with a bow, since they don't have a ranged attack.

## Lore

The Rivendell Elves are Noldorin Elves who dwell in Rivendell, home of the Lord Elrond. They are more ancient than the Wood-Elves and Galadhrim, and are great warriors and loremasters.
