# Cheese Curds

## Overview

Cheese curds are a pitiful food source and an intermitted step of the cheese production process.

## Obtaining

Obtained by cooking a [Milk Bucket](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Milk_Bucket) in a furnace to obtain a Bucket of Cheese Curds. Right clicking with the Bucket of Cheese Curds in the players main hand will result in the cheese curds separating from the bucket. 
### Uses

Used to craft [[cheese_wheel|Cheese Wheels]]
{{crafting:cheese_wheel}}

## Trivia

Prior to [[version#beta-1-7-4|1.7.4]], cooking [Milk Bucket](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Milk_Bucket) in a furnace would directly result in cheese curds, consuming the bucket and making this an expensive item. With the introduction of the Bucket of Cheese Curds item, the bucket can now be reclaimed from the recipe process.
