# Blue Mountains

The **Blue Mountains** is an ancient home of the dwarves.

## NPCs
- [[blue_mountains_dwarf|Blue Mountains Dwarves]] spawn in randomly around the Blue Mountains biome and structures
- [[blue_mountains_warrior|Blue Mountains Warriors]] spawn in randomly around the Blue Mountains biome and structures
- [[blue_mountains_archer|Blue Mountains Archers]] spawn in randomly around the Blue Mountains biome and structures
- [[blue_mountains_axe_thrower|Blue Mountains Axe Throwers]] spawn in randomly around the Blue Mountains biome and structures
- [[blue_mountains_commander|Blue Mountains Commanders]] spawn in Blue Mountains Strongholds and Settlement keeps
- [[blue_mountains_bartender|Blue Mountains Bartenders]] spawn in Blue Mountain Settlement taverns
- [[blue_mountains_butcher|Blue Mountains Butchers]] spawn in Blue Mountain Settlement trader halls
- [[blue_mountains_miner|Blue Mountains Miners]] spawn randomly underground
- [[blue_mountains_smith|Blue Mountains Smiths]] spawn in Blue Mountain Settlement forges and in surface smithies
- [[blue_mountains_stonemason|Blue Mountains Stonemasons]] spawn in Blue Mountain Settlement trader halls
- [[blue_mountains_merchant|Blue Mountains Merchants]] are a randomly spawning wandering trader that can be found across Middle Earth
